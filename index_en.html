Discovering Russia

The real country is not in the news reports. It is here.

What is there to see?

According to the Russian Public Opinion Research Center's surveys, 95% of the Russians want to travel, but only 36% want to travel domestically on vacation. Mostly, people do not believe there is anything of interest to see. However, Russia is a whole universe with gentle seas in the south, thick forests of the Sayan Mountains and harsh ice of the Putorana Plateau. This beauty is available to anyone, not millions in cash, travel documents and many hours of flight needed. You can see it, like Vera Bashmakova, a brave young mother, who grabbed her three children, put them into her Lada car and drove 20 thousand kilometers around her home country. We have selected some vacation worthy places and wrote about them.

Curonian Spit

URL park-kosa.ru

Here, in the middle of forests and sand dunes, there are two water horizons: the calm Curonian bay on one side and the rippled Baltic Sea waves on the other. A unique natural zone at the edge of the Russian enclave.

This is not where the Kaliningrad region ends. For a traveler and explorer there is also the westernmost point of Russia, the Baltic Spit, and the German heritage of the small scattered seaside towns. There is no hustle here, only the calmness of nature and the smell of steely and cold sea.

Kola Peninsula

URL National Geographic

Almost the entire peninsula is beyond the Arctic Circle. The Saami tundra, with the taiga to the south and the Arctic Ocean to the north, pretending to be the Barents Sea.

You may have watched films by Zvyagintsev and even heard the history of the Arctic Festival in Teriberka. Perhaps the word "Khibiny" is not buried under the snow of school memories of geography. Perhaps you weren't interested in the super-deep borehole that penetrates the earth's crust, but apathy has long conquered you. But you can make the first step towards bringing your dream of seeing northern lights to life with a ticket to Murmansk.

Altai

URL Facebook

Altai is one of the most beautiful places in Russia. First of all because of the mountains: if you drive along the ridge, you will see slopes covered with pine trees, mountain rivers and lakes. And if you open the windows in the car, you will know the invisible local wonder â€” the mountain air.

The Altai climate is moderate, so it's best to go here in the summer. This way you'll see all the diversity of the local flora and fauna. Elks roam the forests of the Altai, eagles fly over the ridges, and roe deer graze on the plains. And the famous Pallas's cats also inhabit the Altai Krai.

Winter Baikal

URL https://vk.com/baikalmile

Everyone knows Baikal as the largest lake in the world. Many also know that it is the largest source of fresh water and one of the most beautiful places in Russia.

Of course it's all true. But Baikal is still the perfect place for a skijoring competition. It's such a sport, when a skier ties themselves to a motorcycle and the tandem tries to gain the highest speed possible on ice. In March 2019 at the Baikal Mile festival the world record speed of 197.011 km/h was set.

Karelia

URL http://vodlozero.ru

Siberia ends not in the Urals, but in Karelia: the Siberian larch, which makes up the taiga, does not grow west of Vodlozero. And it grows up to 30 meters here, because the forests of Karelian national parks have never been touched by an axe thanks to impassible marshes. Some of the pines are more than half a millennium old. Touch a living creature that saw the sun before Ivan the Terrible did. In the unspoiled forest you can walk for a hundred kilometers and never see a trail. And the ground on the occasional paths is marked with bear claws to show everyone who rules this place.
